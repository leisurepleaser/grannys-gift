Our Story

A heritage lemon–lime 7UP pound cake, baked from one family’s secret.

Granny’s Gift is a lemon-lime 7UP pound cake made from a 50+ year secret family recipe created by Dolores “Granny” Jett. She taught her granddaughter — my mother — who then taught me. Today, only the two of us know the complete recipe and method.

Each cake is mixed and baked by hand in our Washington home kitchen, preserving the same care, patience, and tradition that once anchored Granny’s holiday tables. When you serve Granny’s Gift, you are sharing a slice of family history, nostalgia, and home.
